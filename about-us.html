About Us

Dr. Hari

BAMS

Dr. Hari P N hailing from Kerala completed the 7½ year residential Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery (BAMS) course in 1992 from the Ayurveda College Coimbatore

Apart from academic studies, he was also trained over 19 years in traditional Gurukula education from the age of 10 to 29 years. He is well versed in Sanskrit, English, Malayalam, Tamil and Hindi.

He has worked over 21 years (1992 to 2014) with the renowned Arya Vaidya Pharmacy Coimbatore Ltd as Trustee & Deputy Director of Arsha Yoga Vidya Peetam Trust and the Member of Governing Council of AVREFA, which runs Ayurveda College Coimbatore.

He is currently the Founder & Chief Physician of Sudarsanam Center for Ayurveda and Yoga, Coimbatore, India a Gurukulam for Panchakarma treatment and training in Ayurveda & Yoga since 2014. He is also a visiting faculty in several institutions around the world including Dr. Albert Einstein Institute of Medical Science, Sao Paulo (M.D Integrative Medicine) and Mt. Madonna Institute of Ayurveda, California etc.

He has to-date treated more than 65,000 patients in India and internationally mainly on Skin diseases, Rheumatoid Arthritis, Hypertension, GERD, IBS, Ulcerative colitis, Multiple sclerosis, Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis, Guillain barre syndrome, Thyroid diseases and Cancers etc.

He has conducted more than 60 seminars, conferences and training programs in various countries.

He has lectured over 40,000 hours to International students including Hollywood celebrities, specialists in modern medicine and other leading intellectuals. He is a frequent International traveller and has been instrumental in introducing Ayurveda as an approved system in National Certification Board for Therapeutic Massage and Body Work (NCBTMB), USA.

Dr. Devan

PH

Dr. Devan PH is a fourth-generation Ayurvedic scholar and practitioner, carrying forward a rich legacy of traditional healing and holistic wellness. With 3 years of dedicated clinical experience, he has earned a reputation for his compassionate care and effective, evidence-based Ayurvedic treatments. He's passionately propagating Ayurveda to the world.

He graduated from the esteemed Ayurveda College, Coimbatore, and is currently pursuing an MBA at Manipal University. Dr. Devan currently practices at Sudarsanam Ayurveda, where he consults patients from across India and abroad. His expertise spans a wide range of health conditions, with a special focus on:

  • 1. Liver Disorders
  • 2. Pancreatitis
  • 3. Arthritis & Pain Management
  • 4. Panchakarma Detoxification Therapies
  • 5. Skin Diseases
  • 6. Gallstones & Digestive Ailments
  • 7. Joint Pains

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Devan is a passionate practitioner and instructor of yoga and Kalaripayattu (traditional martial art of Kerala). He has conducted numerous yoga and kalari workshops for foreign students, both online and offline classes helping them discover the healing power of India’s ancient wisdom. Known for his personalized and integrative approach, Dr. Devan seamlessly blends classical Ayurvedic principles with modern lifestyle practices, aiming to restore balance, strengthen immunity, and improve overall well-being in his patients.

Vision

At Sudarsanam Ayurveda Clinic, our vision is to be a global leader in authentic Ayurvedic healing, combining the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda with modern wellness practices. We strive to promote holistic health, prevent lifestyle diseases, and guide individuals toward a balanced, natural, and disease-free life. Through compassionate care, personalized therapies, and educational excellence, we aim to be a center of excellence for Ayurvedic treatments, Panchakarma, and wellness education—trusted locally and respected globally.
